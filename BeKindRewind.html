About Us

This production company is an outlet for the creative endeavors of Chris Perez Perhaps one day it can represent more people, maybe it's pointless. Nonetheless, this is my attempt to have my shit together.

I was born during the actualization of digital media, so I was able to develope a deep apreciation for physical media. I witnessed the abandonment of video casset tapes and later CD's. Although it was easy to move beyond these artifacts and their limitations, they have grown to represent a simpler time albeit more flawed era.

I observed as imperfections in videos and pictures became en vogue, with apps like Instagram and VSCO dominating the photo editing space during my teenage years. I participated in Instagram myself, wrongly believing that the application was only a photo editor. It wasn't until after the SOCIAL MEDIA became popular in my highscool that I realized everyone could see my photos, even the ones that were rough drafts of what I actually posted on Facebook, the dominant social media at the time. See how I already sound like a Boomer?
